Dowload the repo and run Game2D.exe from the folder Build to play the game.

Use Arrow keys to control the Balls.

Constraints: 
1. The Arrow keys control the black and red balls simultaneously.
2. Both the balls cannot go out of bounds or hit each other.
3. The red ball cannot pick up the coin.

How to win:
1. Avoid the constraints, pick up the coin with the black ball and get out of the Play Zone through the left boundary close to the coin.
